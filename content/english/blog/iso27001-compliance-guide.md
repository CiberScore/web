## What is ISO27001?

**ISO27001** is the international standard that specifies requirements for establishing, implementing, maintaining and continually improving an Information Security Management System (ISMS).

This certification demonstrates that your organization has implemented systematic controls to protect confidential information.

## Benefits of ISO27001 certification

### For your business:
- **Customer trust** and competitive advantage
- **Risk reduction** in security
- **Regulatory compliance** with regulations
- **Process improvement** internal
- **Reputation protection**

### For your employees:
- **Security awareness**
- **Clear and documented** processes
- **Organizational security** culture

## ISO27001 certification process

### 1. Initial analysis
- Current state assessment
- Gap identification
- Project planning

### 2. ISMS implementation
- Scope definition
- Risk assessment
- Control implementation
- Process documentation

### 3. Certification audit
- Internal audit
- External audit (stage 1 and 2)
- Certificate obtaining

## Key ISO27001 controls

The **93 controls** from Annex A cover:

- **Security policies**
- **Asset management**
- **Access control**
- **Cryptography**
- **Physical security**
- **Incident management**
- **Business continuity**

## Certification maintenance

- **Annual surveillance** audits
- **Management reviews**
- **Continuous improvement** of ISMS
- **Renewal** every 3 years
